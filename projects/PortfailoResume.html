Portfailo & Resume

Introduction

In today’s fast-paced professional world, having an online portfolio and a resume is a must for anyone looking to make a mark. This project was designed to offer a seamless experience where users can manage their portfolio and resume from a single place, making it easier to showcase their skills, projects, and experience, both digitally and in print

Features

Resume Generation

The content from the portfolio is automatically transferred to a resume format. A clean, structured, and ready-to-print resume is just a click away. This feature is particularly useful for job applications, interviews, and professional networking.

Cool Design

The overall design aesthetic is contemporary and elegant. With clean lines, smooth animations, and a balance between functionality and creativity, the interface feels intuitive yet stunning

Mode Night

The web app includes a dark mode feature, designed to reduce eye strain during late-night work sessions while maintaining a sleek and modern design. Whether you're browsing during the day or the evening, the night mode ensures a clean, minimalistic look that adapts to any time of day.
